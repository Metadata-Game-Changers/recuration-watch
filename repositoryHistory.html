Completeness, year by year

Samples a repository’s records for each registered year, scores every year against the MGC FAIR use cases, and plots how completeness evolves. Each year’s score reflects those records as they are today — including any re-curation since.

Completeness by registered year

Each point scores a sample of the records registered in that year, as those records are today — so a strong early year can mean good metadata then or re-curation since (compare with Re-Curation Watch). Thin years (fewer than 10 records) — hollow points in Lines, dimmed cells in Grid — have noisy percentages. Results appear as each year finishes; switch View to a radar-plot Grid to watch each use case’s concept profile change year to year.

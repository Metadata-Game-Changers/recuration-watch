Find ROR identifiers for affiliationsprototype

Paste organization names or affiliation strings (one per line). Each is matched against the Research Organization Registry using the ROR affiliation strategy — the same algorithm behind MGC’s RORRetriever tool. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded or stored.

Paste affiliations (one per line) and click Find RORs.

ROR matches

Rows are colored by outcome: green = a ROR was chosen by the matching algorithm, amber = a best-score match accepted without being chosen (only with the option above), red = no match. Affiliations the matcher can’t place are automatically retried with ror.org’s keyword search (the website’s more forgiving query); its top hit appears in amber with QUERY in the Match column — verify those by eye. Click an affiliation or its result count to see every candidate the API returned. The CSV download matches the RORRetriever.py output columns.
